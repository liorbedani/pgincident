Privacy

PgIncident is designed to analyze PostgreSQL incidents and return a diagnostic response.

What you submit

When you use the service, you may submit PostgreSQL errors, logs, operating system details, and version information.

How data is used

Submitted data is used to generate the analysis response. Do not paste secrets, credentials, private keys, or data you are not allowed to share.

Storage

The app is intended to minimize retained data. However, infrastructure providers, platform logs, or third-party AI providers may process request data as part of delivering the service.

Recommended usage

Review all output before applying commands in production. The service provides guidance, not guaranteed correctness.
